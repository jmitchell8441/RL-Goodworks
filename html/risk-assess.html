Vulnerability and Risk Assessment

Risk management is critical to an organization’s overall security posture. Risk must be identified, tracked, and must be adequately accepted by key decision-makers within an organization. A vulnerability and risk mitigation strategy must align resources that will effectively manage the identified Plan of Action and Milestones (POAMs). RL Goodworks understands that risk cannot be eliminated entirely. Our risk management solutions consider cost, resources, and the organization’s mission.

Our Consultants and Engineers use a results-driven approach in every engagement. We work with our clients to identify, understand, and mitigate risks and vulnerabilities within their environments. An engagement involves leadership buy-in, data gathering and collection, identification of vulnerabilities and threats, and finally, the recommendations phase. Recommendations are based on industry best practices, cost, and the alignment of those elements with the organization’s mission.
