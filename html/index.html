About Us

The Information Technology (IT) revolution and the proliferation of the Internet have created new enemies; such as hackers, insider threat, and those with malicious intent. The enemy takes advantage of technological and human vulnerabilities to exploit critical data systems. Proactive planning and aggressive counter measures provide assurance of an organization’s data and its IT assets.

RL Goodworks uses a concept coined High Performance Engineering that engages people, processes and technology to meet and exceed security objectives. Our High Performance Engineers assist our clients to remove the mystery from the mounds of security related documentation, required for the Federal Information Security Management Act (FISMA), and organizational compliance. The best way to assess an organization’s security posture is with a purposed approached that identifies both the organization’s strengths and its weaknesses. Our Engineers and Consultants have the experience and expertise to assist clients in many areas of the Information Assurance discipline. One High Performance Engineer can perform a number of security related tasks from penetration testing, to performing a System Test and Evaluation (ST&E) as part of a Certification and Accreditation (C&A). Creating a user-friendly environment that is compliant with organizational polices, operational guidelines, public law and government mandate is a realistic goal that we can help you reach. Our security focused service offerings include: Vulnerability/Risk Assessment, Design Engineering, Security Awareness & Training, Compliance, and Security Operations.

Our Services

The process of identifying our clients’ required security services begins with the fundamental ideology behind the concept of Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability (CIA). These components represent the key security requirements of any information technology (IT) system, and are extremely important with regard to risk analysis. A quality approach to information security involves protecting organizational data in accordance with appropriate guidelines, standards, policy, and public law. RL Goodworks’ core competencies are designed to focus on five key areas of Information Assurance: Vulnerability & Risk Assessment, Design Engineering, Security Awareness Training, Compliance, and Security Operations.
